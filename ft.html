FT Flume

  • MAJI INFRA Precast FT Flume is a large U Shape Drain designed as water way product. FT Flume is a
    longitudinal drain and its waffle walls are designed JIS T-25 i.e. 10 KN/m2 live load.
  • FT Flume is available in the following sizes.
    Width: 900 ~ 2000 mm
    Height: 900 ~ 1800 mm
  • Pun on Lid of FT Flume is designed for JIS (T-4) i.e. for pedestrian load and it is available in
    Standard Lid & No Hole option.
  • FT Flume U Shape can also be used as Utility Duct / Cable Duct.
